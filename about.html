About

Isabella Mcgaugh

I am Isabella Mcgaugh, a graphic designer and UX-focused visual storyteller based in Knoxville, Tennessee. My work blends editorial design, motion, and interactive web experiences to create outcomes that are clear and expressive.

I am currently studying Graphic Design at the University of Tennessee, where I build user-centered digital work rooted in strong typographic systems, visual identity, and thoughtful interaction.

Tools: Figma, InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Premiere, Lightroom, Procreate.
